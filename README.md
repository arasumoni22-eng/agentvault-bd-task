## 1. Target User Segments

### Segment A — Active Retail Crypto Traders (day/swing traders on CEX & DEX)

- **Why them:** They trade daily, feel market volatility directly, and are the fastest group to try a new edge-giving tool — short sales cycle, high word-of-mouth in trading communities.
- **Pain point AgentVault solves:** They can't watch charts 24/7, miss entries/exits, and trade emotionally under pressure. An AI assistant that monitors markets and flags/executes signals removes the fatigue and emotion from their process.
- **Where to find them:** Crypto Twitter/X (trading threads, #cryptotrading), Discord servers of exchanges and trading communities, r/CryptoCurrency and r/CryptoMarkets, TradingView chat, Telegram signal groups.

### Segment B — DAO Treasurers & Small DeFi Fund Managers

- **Why them:** They manage pooled, often meaningful capital, are held accountable to a community, and actively look for tools that reduce operational risk — a strong wedge into a network of other DAOs and funds if the first one converts.
- **Pain point AgentVault solves:** They juggle multiple protocols and positions manually, and a missed rebalance or risk signal is a visible, costly mistake. AgentVault gives always-on monitoring and faster, less error-prone decisions across a treasury.
- **Where to find them:** DAO governance forums (Snapshot, Commonwealth, Discourse), DAO Discord servers (governance/treasury channels), LinkedIn ("DAO Treasurer", "DeFi Fund Manager" titles), DeFi conferences and Twitter Spaces.

## 2. Outreach Example

**Channel:** X (Twitter) DM — targeting an active retail crypto trader

> Hey [Name] — saw your take on the ETH move today, sharp read.
>
> I'm with AgentVault — we're building an AI trading assistant that watches the market 24/7 and flags entries/exits so you're not glued to charts all day. We're at MVP and onboarding our first 50 traders for early access, free, in exchange for honest feedback.
>
> Would you be up for a 10-min look? Happy to set you up with a live account, no pitch deck needed.
>
> — [Your name], AgentVault

*Why this works: opens with a genuine, specific hook (not generic praise), states the value prop in one line, makes the ask small (10 minutes) and low-friction (free early access, no deck), and matches the casual tone of the platform.*

## 3. GTM Ideas to Get the First 50 Users

1. **University Blockchain Club Beta Drop** — DM 10–15 university blockchain/fintech club Discords, offer their members exclusive early access plus a small credit for the first 5 who give structured feedback. Students are vocal on Twitter and reused as a case study.
2. **"Beat the Bot" Trading Challenge** — A one-week testnet leaderboard where traders compete against AgentVault's own signals. Low cost, inherently shareable on Twitter/Discord, and doubles as a public demo of the product actually working.
3. **DAO Pilot Partnerships** — Offer 3–5 small DAOs a free, white-glove pilot for their treasury monitoring in exchange for a public testimonial or governance-forum post. One credible DAO endorsement carries a lot of trust into other DAOs.
4. **Crypto Meme Contest (stretch idea)** — Run a light meme contest around "trading without sleep" with early-access spots as prizes, to build top-of-funnel awareness cheaply on Twitter.
